👥 Who We Are

Quick Do PDF was founded by a dedicated team of software engineers and privacy advocates who were frustrated by the lack of secure, free document tools on the internet. We noticed that most major PDF services require users to upload their highly sensitive data (like bank statements, legal contracts, and medical records) to unknown third-party cloud servers just to perform basic file edits.

We built Quick Do PDF to fundamentally change that paradigm. By leveraging modern client-side WebAssembly and JavaScript technologies, our dedicated development team ensures that you have enterprise-grade PDF manipulation power directly within your own local browser—meaning your documents never leave your physical device.

🛡️ Why Trust Us? (Our Core Values)

We believe that trust has to be earned through transparency and architectural design, not just promises. Here is our ongoing commitment to our users:

  • Zero Data Retention: Because no file is ever uploaded to our servers, there is absolutely zero risk of your documents being hacked, leaked, or intercepted on our end.
  • No Tracking Analytics on Documents: While we use standard analytical tools to see which pages are popular, we employ zero tracking code inside your documents or the processing pipeline.
  • No Hidden Paywalls: We do not hold your final processed document hostage behind a surprise "Premium" subscription. Every tool listed on our site is 100% free and fully unlocked.
  • Accessibility First: We continuously audit our platform to ensure it meets strict web accessibility standards (WCAG), making our tools usable for everyone, regardless of their device or physical constraints.

Our Mission and Vision

At Quick Do PDF, our primary mission is to democratize digital document management. For decades, manipulating PDF files required expensive, heavy desktop software subscriptions or forced users to upload their sensitive and confidential data to remote cloud servers. We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right, and that document processing should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill or financial status. Our vision is a web where users can fully control their data locally without compromising on power or utility.

How We Built Quick Do PDF

Built by a dedicated team of web developers and privacy advocates based in New Delhi, India, Quick Do PDF utilizes cutting-edge web technologies like HTML5 Canvas and WebAssembly. These modern browser APIs allow us to run complex arithmetic and file conversions directly inside your device's RAM. Because the heavy lifting happens locally, we have managed to completely eliminate the need for costly backend processing servers. This extraordinary efficiency is exactly why we can offer our entire premium suite of over 20 PDF tools completely for free, forever.

Our Story

Quick Do PDF was born out of frustration. Every time we needed to merge a couple of PDFs, compress a file for email, or quickly split a document, we'd be directed to websites that demanded account creation, showed intrusive ads, placed watermarks on our files, or — worst of all — uploaded our sensitive documents to remote servers.

We believed there had to be a better way. In 2023, we built the first version of Quick Do PDF: a simple, client-side PDF toolkit that runs entirely in your browser. No servers. No uploads. No accounts. No watermarks. Just tools that work.

What We Stand For
