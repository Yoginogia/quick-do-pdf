🎯 Our Mission

We believe PDF tools should be free, fast, and private. Most online PDF tools upload your sensitive documents to their servers — bank statements, contracts, personal files. That's a privacy risk.

Quick Do PDF is different. Every single operation happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We can't see them, we don't store them, and we never will.

🛠️ Our Tools

Quick Do PDF offers a comprehensive suite of PDF tools:

Edit: Merge, Split, Rotate, Crop, Delete Pages, Reorder, Add Page Numbers, Add Watermark, Add Background

Convert: PDF to Word, PDF to Excel, PDF to JPG, JPG to PDF, Word to PDF

Security: Protect PDF, Unlock PDF, Sign PDF, Flatten, Sanitize

Optimize: Compress, Grayscale, Extract Text, Extract Images, OCR, Compare PDFs

🔧 Technology

Quick Do PDF is built with modern web technologies and industry-standard open-source libraries including pdf.js (by Mozilla), pdf-lib, and jsPDF. All processing is powered by JavaScript running in your browser's sandbox environment.
