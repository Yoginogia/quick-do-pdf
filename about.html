🎯 Our Mission

We believe PDF tools should be free, fast, and private. Most online PDF tools upload your sensitive documents to their servers — bank statements, contracts, personal files. That's a privacy risk.

Quick Do PDF is different. Every single operation happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We can't see them, we don't store them, and we never will.

👥 Who We Are

Quick Do PDF was founded by a dedicated team of software engineers and privacy advocates who were frustrated by the lack of secure, free document tools on the internet. We noticed that most major PDF services require users to upload their highly sensitive data (like bank statements, legal contracts, and medical records) to unknown third-party cloud servers just to perform basic file edits.

We built Quick Do PDF to fundamentally change that paradigm. By leveraging modern client-side WebAssembly and JavaScript technologies, our dedicated development team ensures that you have enterprise-grade PDF manipulation power directly within your own local browser—meaning your documents never leave your physical device.

🛡️ Why Trust Us? (Our Core Values)

We believe that trust has to be earned through transparency and architectural design, not just promises. Here is our ongoing commitment to our users:

  • Zero Data Retention: Because no file is ever uploaded to our servers, there is absolutely zero risk of your documents being hacked, leaked, or intercepted on our end.
  • No Tracking Analytics on Documents: While we use standard analytical tools to see which pages are popular, we employ zero tracking code inside your documents or the processing pipeline.
  • No Hidden Paywalls: We do not hold your final processed document hostage behind a surprise "Premium" subscription. Every tool listed on our site is 100% free and fully unlocked.
  • Accessibility First: We continuously audit our platform to ensure it meets strict web accessibility standards (WCAG), making our tools usable for everyone, regardless of their device or physical constraints.

🛠️ Our Tools

Quick Do PDF offers a comprehensive suite of PDF tools:

Edit: Merge, Split, Rotate, Crop, Delete Pages, Reorder, Add Page Numbers, Add Watermark, Add Background

Convert: PDF to Word, PDF to Excel, PDF to JPG, JPG to PDF, Word to PDF

Security: Protect PDF, Unlock PDF, Sign PDF, Flatten, Sanitize

Optimize: Compress, Grayscale, Extract Text, Extract Images, OCR, Compare PDFs

🔧 Technology

Quick Do PDF is built with modern web technologies and industry-standard open-source libraries including pdf.js (by Mozilla), pdf-lib, and jsPDF. All processing is powered by JavaScript running in your browser's sandbox environment.
